Who we are

Sport as a Tool for Change

Formerly known as RERNCO Sports Academy, Sports Bridge International Foundation was established to create inclusive sporting ecosystems where young people, underserved communities, and migrants can thrive.

We believe sport has the unique ability to:

  • Unite people across cultures
  • Build confidence and resilience
  • Create access to education
  • Improve mental and physical wellbeing
  • Develop employability skills
  • Strengthen social cohesion

Our mission is simple: to leverage sport as a platform for social impact, economic empowerment, and cross-cultural integration.

03

Flagship Programme

Sport Without Borders.

A flagship initiative supporting migrant communities through sport as a means of integration, healing, and belonging. Sport is one of the most powerful tools for integration — helping migrants build relationships, learn the culture, and feel a genuine sense of belonging. By bringing migrants and host communities together on the field and court, we turn shared play into shared community.

“What we launched in Lagos is more than a sports facility; it is a scalable platform that merges sport, enterprise, and social impact in a way that reflects the future of community development.” Rerhe Idonije,
Managing Partner, Sports Bridge International

A recent tournament brought together players of many nationalities and professional backgrounds — underscoring sport's role as a platform for networking, wellness, and international exchange.

What's next
  • Tournaments designed specifically for migrant communities
  • Workshops for mothers and female migrants
  • Multi-sport pathways — golf, badminton, swimming and more — as tools for finding friends, learning the culture, and integrating into society
  • Support that goes beyond the social: helping people discover new ambitions, find work, and support their mental health

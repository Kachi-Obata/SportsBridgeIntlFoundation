Behind every figure is a young person who found a coach, a teammate, a mentor, or a first real opportunity. Since 2023, we've grown from a single academy into a social enterprise reaching across more than 15 communities in Nigeria — proving that sport, paired with education and mentorship, is one of the most effective tools for social change.

02

Our approach

Our Approach to Impact

We measure success not just in participation, but in transformation: skills gained, confidence built, mentors connected, and barriers removed. Sport is the entry point; lasting empowerment is the goal.

  • Increased youth participation in sport
  • Better access to education
  • Greater inclusion for migrants
  • Improved community wellbeing
  • Employment pathways for young people
  • Stronger social cohesion

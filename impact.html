Our approach

How we measure what we do

We track inputs, outputs and outcomes — not just activity, but lasting change. Our framework is built on three questions.

Did we reach the right young people? We disaggregate every figure by gender, age and community to ensure we're serving those who need us most — not just those easiest to reach.

Did they progress? We follow participants along the pathway, measuring transitions from gaming to sport, from sport to development, and from development to education or employment.

Did it last? Through follow-up surveys and partner data, we track scholarship retention, career outcomes and the rate at which graduates return to coach — our truest measure of success.

Every figure on this page is collected through programme registration, attendance records, partner-school reporting and annual outcome surveys. We publish an annual impact report and welcome independent verification from our partners.

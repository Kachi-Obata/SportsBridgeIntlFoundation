Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

01 / Who we are

About this policy

The Sports Bridge International Foundation ("the Foundation", "we", "us") is the social impact arm of Sports Bridge International, based at Ikoyi Club 1938, Awolowo Road, Ikoyi, Lagos, Nigeria. This policy explains, in plain language, what personal information we collect through this website, why we collect it, and how we look after it.

02 / What we collect

Information you give us

We only collect the personal information you choose to share with us:

  • Contact form: your name, email address, phone number (optional), the reason for your enquiry, and your message.
  • Newsletter sign-up: your email address.

We do not collect personal information from children through this website. Programme enrolment is completed with a parent or guardian.

03 / How we use it

What we do with your information

  • Respond to your enquiry and follow up where needed.
  • Send you our newsletter, if you’ve asked for it. You can unsubscribe at any time.
  • Keep honest records of our work, as required by law.

We never sell your personal information, and we never share it with third parties for their marketing.

04 / Cookies

Cookies & analytics

This website does not currently set cookies or run analytics. If we add privacy-respecting analytics in the future, we will update this policy first and tell you what is measured and why. The embedded Google Map on our contact page may set its own cookies, governed by Google’s policies.

05 / Your rights

Access, correction and deletion

You can ask us at any time to see the personal information we hold about you, to correct it, or to delete it. Email info@sportsbridgeintl.com and we will respond within 30 days. We process personal information in line with the Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023.

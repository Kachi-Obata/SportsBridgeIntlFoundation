What we do

Programmes that meet young people where they are — and take them further than they imagined.

Each programme is a stop on the Sports Bridge pathway. Together they form one connected journey: from a phone screen to a pitch, from a pitch to a podium, from a podium to a profession.

01

Grassroots Academies

Free after-school and weekend academies across Lagos — football, basketball, athletics and more, led by certified coaches with a duty of care.

28 academies · 6 sports · Free for every child

02

The Bridge Gaming League

Our signature innovation — safe, structured e-sports that bridges young people into physical sport.

3,400 gamers engaged · 41% transition to physical sport

03

Girls on the Ball

Dismantling the barriers that keep girls off the pitch, with female coaches, safe spaces, free kit and leadership development.

40% of athletes are girls · 6 communities

04

School Leagues & Scholarships

Inter-school competitions paired with academic scholarships.

16 partner schools · 220+ scholarships awarded

05

Estate Tournaments

Pop-up community tournaments that identify talent and put equipment into local hands.

48 tournaments · 20k+ spectators & players

06

Coach & Career Academy

Vocational training in coaching, officiating, sports media and event management.

120 trained · 70% in sport-linked work or study

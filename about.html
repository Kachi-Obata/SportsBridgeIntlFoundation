Our story

Born from a vision to bridge social divides through the universal power of sport.

Sports Bridge International Foundation was born out of a vision to bridge social divides through the universal power of sport.

Evolving from RERNCO Sports Academy, the Foundation was created to expand its impact beyond sports participation into broader areas of social inclusion, youth empowerment, migrant integration, and community transformation.

We recognise that millions of young people around the world — especially those from underserved backgrounds and migrant communities — face barriers to access, belonging, and opportunity. Sport can break those barriers.

By combining sport, mentorship, education, and enterprise, we create practical pathways that empower individuals and strengthen communities.

Our Evolution

What began as RERNCO Sports Academy has matured into Sports Bridge International Foundation — a broader vision reflecting our expanded mission. The name change marks our commitment to building bridges: between people and opportunity, between cultures, and between underserved communities and the resources they deserve.

Mission & vision

Why we exist

Mission

To bridge the gap between sport, social inclusion, and economic opportunity by creating sustainable pathways for youth, women, migrants, and underserved communities through sport, education, mentorship, and enterprise.

Vision

To become a globally recognised platform where sport drives inclusion, opportunity, and social transformation for underserved and migrant communities.

“Sport can break those barriers.”

Why Sport?

Sport breaks down barriers that words cannot. It brings together people from different backgrounds, builds discipline and resilience, and creates natural opportunities for mentorship and growth. For migrant populations and underserved communities, sport offers something powerful: a level field, a sense of belonging, and a pathway to broader opportunity.
